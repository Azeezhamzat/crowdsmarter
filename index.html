Our Methodology

The CLEAR Framework

A scientifically-grounded approach to unlocking your organization's collective intelligence. Each step is designed to overcome specific barriers to group wisdom.

C

Capture All Voices

Ensure every participant contributes before discussion begins. Written before spoken.

L

Level the Playing Field

Neutralize authority bias and dominant personalities. Ideas compete, not titles.

E

Expose Hidden Assumptions

Surface unstated beliefs and biases that distort decisions. Make the invisible visible.

A

Aggregate Intelligently

Combine individual judgments using proven mathematical methods. Wisdom emerges.

R

Reflect and Learn

Build organizational CI capability over time. Every decision teaches the next.
